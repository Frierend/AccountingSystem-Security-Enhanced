# IT16 PDF Formatting Guide

## Purpose

This guide supports conversion of IT16_SECURITY_DOCUMENTATION.md from Markdown to PDF for school submission. The Markdown file contains the required content and order. The optional IT16_PRINT_STYLE.css file provides print styling when the Markdown-to-PDF converter supports custom CSS.

## Recommended Conversion

- Convert IT16_SECURITY_DOCUMENTATION.md to PDF.
- Use IT16_PRINT_STYLE.css during conversion if the converter supports custom CSS.
- Keep the output simple, black-and-white, and school-document friendly.
- Do not add decorative formatting, colored text, emojis, or special bullet symbols.

## Target Formatting

- Paper size: A4
- Margins: 1 inch on all sides
- Body font: Arial, 12 pt
- Main headings: Arial, 14 pt, bold
- Subheadings: Arial, 13 pt, bold
- Font color: black only
- Text alignment: justified where applicable
- Line spacing: 1.15
- Tables: simple borders, black text, readable spacing
- Screenshot placeholders: clearly labeled

## Pre-Submission Checklist

- Verify A4 paper size.
- Verify 1-inch margins.
- Verify Arial 12 pt body text.
- Verify 14 pt headings.
- Verify 13 pt subheadings.
- Verify black text only.
- Verify justified paragraphs.
- Verify proper spacing.
- Verify tables are readable and not awkwardly split when possible.
- Verify there are no broken screenshot placeholders.
- Verify there are no duplicated evidence placeholders.
- Verify screenshots do not show visible secrets, OTPs, recovery codes, passwords, or .env values.

## Screenshot Safety Reminder

- Blur OTP codes.
- Blur recovery codes.
- Do not show .env.
- Do not show SMTP password.
- Do not show PayMongo secret key.
- Do not show JWT secret.
- Do not show reCAPTCHA secret key.
- Do not show real passwords.
- Public reCAPTCHA site keys may appear because they are client-side public values.
